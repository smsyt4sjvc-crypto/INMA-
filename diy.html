Common questions

FAQ

What kinds of projects do you help with?

Siding (Hardie, LP, vinyl, wood), window replacement, exterior doors, trim, fascia, soffit, exterior painting. Basically anything on the outside of your house. If it's something I'd normally build, I can coach you through it.

How fast can you get me a house visit?

Usually within the same week. Call or text me — I'll tell you straight.

What if I start and realize I don't want to finish it?

No problem. I can either finish the job myself or bring in one of my vetted contractors to take over. You don't lose the money you've already spent on materials — we just pick up where you left off.

Do I need special tools?

For most exterior work: a good circular saw, a decent drill, a level, a chop saw, and a ladder or scaffold. I'll tell you if your project needs anything specialty (like a siding nailer) and whether to rent or buy.

Can I just call you once for a specific question?

Happy to do one-off consults for a flat $75 phone call, no house visit needed. Text me what you're working on.

What's your service area?

Spokane, Spokane Valley, Liberty Lake, Cheney, Mead, Post Falls, and Coeur d'Alene. Outside that area, add mileage.

Do you sell the materials?

No. I route you to the right supplier — either I pick them up for you (at cost + handling) or you pick them up yourself. I make nothing on the materials, so there's no incentive to upsell you.

Ready to start?

Text a photo of your project. I'll tell you if DIY makes sense for it — straight up, no pressure.
