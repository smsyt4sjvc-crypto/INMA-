How to read this: This is a deliberately simplified teaching model of one thin-margin business in a competitive market — not an economic forecast, and not the whole economy. Real outcomes depend on industry, region, productivity, and a hundred things no slider captures. The point isn't the exact numbers; it's the shape of the tradeoffs — that magnitude and context decide whether a good-sounding policy helps or backfires. Built to argue with, not to believe.
