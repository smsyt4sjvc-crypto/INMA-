What Is This Number?

The number you entered is the Producer Price Index for Construction (PPI), published by the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics and tracked by the Federal Reserve. It measures what contractors pay for building materials, labor, equipment, and overhead — before those costs get passed to you.

Our calculator compares the current value to a baseline average. When the number is higher than the baseline, costs are elevated. When it's lower, you're in a favorable pricing window. The score translates that gap into a simple 1-10 scale.

The baseline is set from recent 12-month averages and is updated periodically to stay accurate.

Disclaimer: The PPI data comes from the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics via the Federal Reserve (FRED). The score is calculated from the entered value relative to a stored baseline average.
