### Type & logo
- [2026-07-10] Fonts: **Outfit** (sans) + **DM Serif Display** (display serif),
  Google Fonts. Logo: hand-painted white bear on red (`bear-logo.jpg`). Vibe:
  dark warm-brown backgrounds, hand-painted gold accents, cream text. Avoid
  corporate blue, neon, pure black.

### Document brand kit (print/PDF)
- [2026-07-10] Letter size, ~0.4–0.55in margins, white/`#FFFDF8` sheet, `#1C1A17`
  ink. Print-safe colors: Ink `#1C1A17`, Gold `#B8941F`, Cream `#F5F2ED`/`#FDF8EE`,
  hairline `#E4DED2`, muted `#777`, footer gray `#8A857E`, ruled line `#DAD3C1`.
- [2026-07-10] Document body/tables use `'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif`
  (clean print); DM Serif Display reserved for display/marketing, not form bodies.
- [2026-07-10] Letterhead: bear logo 64px rounded → **INMA** wordmark (800, gold,
  letterspaced) → tagline → 2.5–3px divider. Section header bars are black
  (`#1C1A17`) with cream letterspaced caps.
- [2026-07-10] Totals block order (the fair-market structure): **Subtotal → INMA
  Services (__%, deducted) → New Subtotal + Sales Tax → Contractor Total**
  (bold gold, highlighted). Permitting footnote verbatim: "* Contractor may add
  permitting costs to the final invoice with attached receipts. This is standard
  practice, as each job's costs are based on valuations."
- [2026-07-10] Footer verbatim: "INMA · Inland Northwest Marketing Affiliates ·
  Spokane, WA · WA UBI #606 027 063 · inmagent.com · (509) 251-7792".

### Rules of thumb
- [2026-07-10] Never force required fields — every document generates blank so it
  can be filled by hand. Logos in print windows must be embedded/served from
  `/bear-logo.jpg` (cross-origin URLs fail in iOS PWA/about:blank print).
