# RAW — Hardie Statement Collection palette
_Saved 2026-07-22. Source: James Hardie "The Statement Collection" PDF (jameshardie.com), provided by Jake. Evidence locker — not edited._

Standard ColorPlus palette (curated by Hardie), samples available in Select
Cedarmill texture:

Arctic White · Cobble Stone · Navajo Beige · Khaki Brown · Timber Bark ·
Rich Espresso · Light Mist · Pearl Gray · Gray Slate · Aged Pewter · Iron Gray ·
Night Gray · Midnight Black · Boothbay Blue · Evening Blue · Deep Ocean.

Key fact (Jake): **Arctic White is the only ColorPlus color Hardie paints
made-to-stock (no special order).** Every other color — Statement Collection
included — is a factory special-order. ColorPlus is available in any Hardie
color; "white only" refers to no-special-order stock, not availability.
Hardie also references a broader "Dream Collection" palette.
