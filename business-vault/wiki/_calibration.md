# _calibration — the owner's bias map (fill honestly; this is what makes pushback useful)

Prompts to answer in a working session:
- When do I misprice? (cash-tight months? big jobs? repeat customers?)
- What jobs do I say yes to that I shouldn't?
- Which suppliers/sources do I over-trust?
- What do I put off? (collections? callbacks? paperwork?)
- What would a skeptical partner push back on that nobody currently does?
