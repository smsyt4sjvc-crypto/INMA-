## PJM capacity auction (the scarcity-pricing signal)
- Capacity prices hit the **price cap ~$329–333/MW-day**, a record for the THIRD auction in a row.
- Cumulative move: 2025/26 was ~+800% vs 2024/25; then +22% on top for the RTO zones.
- Beneficiaries (merchant generators clearing the auction): Talen (TLN), Constellation (CEG),
  NRG, Vistra (VST), Public Service Enterprise Group (PSEG).
  - CEG cleared ~17,950–18,025 MW; VST cleared ~10,314–10,566 MW.
- Mechanism note: DOE §202(c) emergency orders FORCE plants to dispatch with cost recovery —
  not a subsidy windfall. The real upside is scarcity/capacity pricing captured by merchants.
