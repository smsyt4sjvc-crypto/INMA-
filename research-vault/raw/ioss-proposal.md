# SOURCE: iOSS — Increased Output Share Structure (Jake Bishop, April 2026)
# Full policy proposal, pasted by author 2026-07-01. Faithful structured capture. His original work.

## One line
Market-based framework to share a defined portion of ABOVE-BASELINE productivity gains with
workers, gains-only, payroll-native, without a wealth tax or a new agency.

## Core mechanic
- When a firm exceeds its approved 3-yr productivity baseline AND clears an operating-profit
  safeguard (dual trigger), the INCREMENTAL above-baseline gain splits **70% firm / 30% workers**.
- Below baseline → no trigger, no obligation. Only outperformance is shared; baseline untouched.

## Baseline
- Rolling 3-yr average of output per labor dollar, same-quarter to same-quarter (seasonality).
  (Earlier drafts: output per operating-expenditure dollar.) Historical+rolling → hard to sandbag.

## Dual trigger (anti-gaming core)
- (1) approved output measure > rolling 3-yr baseline AND (2) operating profit > its own 3-yr
  baseline, same quarter. Prevents payouts from cost-compression / accounting presentation.

## Two lanes
- **Standard Lane**: safe-harbor formulas on existing reporting; 5% materiality band; quarterly
  tax credit for compliance cost; lower audit burden. For most firms.
- **Custom Lane**: firm supplies its own output metric IF auditable, consistently applied, and
  ≥0.7 correlation to audited revenue over 12 quarters; higher audit burden, no materiality band.

## Gain calc
- Current-qtr productivity above approved baseline, applied to relevant labor-dollar base, split 70/30.

## Worker eligibility & distribution
- W-2, primary work location in-state (per quarterly UI filing), wages ≤ 1.5× county median,
  ≥6-mo tenure, ≥250 hrs in qualifying quarter. (Higher-paid salaried excluded — already have
  equity/bonus/profit comp.)
- **Equal per-capita** distribution (deliberately does NOT reproduce wage hierarchy).

## Timing
- Quarter-lag: gains paid the following quarter after verification (anomaly/audit window).
- **No worker clawbacks** ever; true-ups flow through company reserves/refunds/tax credits.

## Worker investment option
- Elect 0–100% of distribution into a 401(k)/IRA-like account, pre-tax, immediate vesting.
  Cash is default. → wealth-building / equity access for workers without equity comp.

## Administration
- Payroll-native; cross-checks SEC 10-Q + state UI wage files + IRS 941; automated anomaly flags
  + human review. Disputed quarters may shift 65/35 during audit, revert to 70/30 on clearance.

## Geographic calibration
- County-median wage standard (Spokane vs King County example). Anti-arbitrage: relocate 50+
  employees → 36-month baseline freeze (carry high-cost baseline; blocks county-shopping).

## Safeguards (8)
Rolling 3-yr baseline; same-quarter comparison; dual trigger; automated SEC/IRS/UI verification;
Standard-Lane materiality band; higher audit burden on custom metrics; no worker clawbacks;
36-mo relocation baseline freeze.

## Implementation
- State-level first; optional Standard Lane + compliance tax credit; pilot firms >250 employees;
  anonymized quarterly reporting to refine. Diffusion analogy: the 401(k) — voluntary practice
  becomes market norm before codification.
