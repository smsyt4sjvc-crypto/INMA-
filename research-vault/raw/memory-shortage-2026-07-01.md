## Confirmation of ongoing/worsening DRAM+NAND shortage
- **Apple raised prices June 25, 2026** across iPads, Macs, HomePods, Vision Pro, Apple TV;
  AAPL fell ~6% (worst day since Apr 2025 crash). (Apple normally absorbs component costs.)
- DRAM +172% in 2025. **Samsung halted new DDR5 orders. Micron EXITED its consumer 'Crucial'
  brand** to redirect capacity to high-margin AI datacenter memory.
- 32GB DDR5 kit: ~$80-120 (2025) → **$374.97** (Jun 2026), ~3-4x. Retail kits sell out in seconds.
- Cause: structural reallocation of fab capacity to AI datacenter (not a 2020-style supply chock).
- Duration forecasts: Gartner ~130% memory cost surge; Kearney PERLab shortage "at least to 2030";
  Micron CEO Mehrotra expects shortage through 2027, gradual improvement 2028.
