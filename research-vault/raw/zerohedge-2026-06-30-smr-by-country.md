## What an SMR is
- Small: ≤300 MWe (vs 1,000-1,400 for traditional reactors).
- Modular: factory mass-manufactured → lower cost + build time.
- 4 reactor types: light-water, fast-neutron, graphite-moderated HTGR, molten-salt.
- Compact → transportable; **"ideal for data centers or remote sites"** where grid connect is costly.

## Framing (as-reported)
- Electricity demand accelerating → SMRs pitched for grid reliability, energy security, "clean firm power."
