# Bloomberg — Williams: AI demand is my top inflation focus; could force hikes

Pasted by Jake 2026-07-10. Bloomberg, Maria Eloisa Capurro, Thu 2026-07-09 7:13 AM PDT.
NY Fed event, Thursday (7/9).

## Key content (verbatim-adjacent)
- NY Fed President John Williams: among US inflation drivers he is "most focused on demand driven by
  artificial intelligence." If that demand persists, "it could force the central bank to raise interest rates."
- Quote: "If this creates a sustained impulse to demand relative to supply in inflation, I do think
  that's the kind of situation where **you don't look through this**." If inflation is more persistent and
  meaningfully higher than baseline, "then monetary policy would need to respond to that."
- Counterpoint kept: if it plays out benign, policy "is, and continues to be, well positioned."
- **June SEP: NINE policymakers penciled in at least one quarter-point HIKE in 2026.** Minutes (released
  Wed 7/8): "a few participants saw a case for raising interest rates."
- Fed has held the benchmark rate steady all year; "support for rate hikes is growing among officials."
