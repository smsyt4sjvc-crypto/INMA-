# InP laser upstream supplier map (Jake's research paste, 2026-07-10)

As-reported from Jake's digest (his other-session research). Confirmations are CLAIMED-confirmed;
not independently verified here.

## The chain
AXT grows InP crystal → cuts/polishes bare wafer → Coherent/Lumentum grow active layers (MOCVD) +
fabricate lasers in-house (they do NOT buy laser chips). Investable upstream = bare wafer + the
machines that grow (MOCVD) and coat (facet deposition) the laser.

## Claimed-confirmed relationships
- **Coherent ← AXT (AXTI):** 3-year development + supply agreement for 6" InP substrates beginning
  2026-06-25; **Coherent PREPAID ~$22.3M to reserve capacity** while AXT expands Beijing production
  through 2028. AXTI ~$3.05B mcap, loss-making; Chinese export permits have repeatedly delayed shipments.
- **Lumentum ← AIXTRON (AIXA/AIXNY):** multiple G10-AsP MOCVD systems bought May 2026 for InP expansion.
  Recipe-qualification lock-in (changing MOCVD vendor risks yield/wavelength/reliability). Nokia and
  SMART Photonics also ordered the platform.
- **Industry ← Veeco (VECO): >$250M InP equipment orders**, deliveries 2026 → accelerating 2027.
  Lumina MOCVD + WaferEtch + **Spector ion-beam facet-coating (substantial share of the $250M)** —
  facet coating = specialized step (bad coating → heat → dead laser). Customers mostly unnamed.
- Lumentum's bare-wafer supplier UNDISCLOSED. Qualified substrate producers: Sumitomo Electric (5802.T),
  JX Advanced Metals (5016.T — **up to ¥120B InP investment over 4 years**), AXT, smaller Asian/private.

## Jake's digest ranking
1. VECO (multi-customer, both machine types, 2027 acceleration; ~$3.5B mcap, distorted trailing P/E)
2. AIXTRON (cleanest confirmed LITE supplier, picks-and-shovels epitaxy)
3. AXTI (most direct scarcity, but repriced + China export-permit control + agreed-pricing contract
   caps spot capture)
4. JX 5016 (conservative substrate exposure, Japan, no confirmed LITE/COHR contract)
