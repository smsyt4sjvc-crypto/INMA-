# ChosunBiz (Koo A-mo, 2026-07-12) — Samsung accelerates Yongin fab #1 to 2029; Honam/Gwangju queued behind

Pasted by Jake 2026-07-12 eve.

## Claims (as-reported)
- **Samsung targeting 2029 operation for fab #1 of SIX planned at the Yongin National Industrial
  Complex — 1-2 years EARLIER than the prior 2030-31 timeline.** Requires expedited land
  compensation, site prep, construction, "large-scale power and water supply facilities."
- Yongin: announced Mar 2023, plan approved Dec 2024, LH land-sale contract Dec 2025. Investment:
  **up to 360 trillion won (~$260B) for the six fabs.**
- **June 30: Samsung announced 425 trillion won (~$310B) for the Honam region, ~400T won of it for
  TWO new fabs in Gwangju.** Yongin prioritized first, then Honam sequentially.
- Samsung statement: "To address the rapidly growing global semiconductor demand, the investment
  timeline... has been significantly accelerated... The timing for preparing a new cluster after
  Yongin has also been moved forward."

  all wafer-layer, all in Korea. Zero of it US.
