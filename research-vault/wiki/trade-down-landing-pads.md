# Trade-Down Landing Pads — who catches the falling customer

The recurring K-shape pattern, formalized 2026-07-10: every price squeeze creates a landing pad one tier
down, and the landing pads outperform while full-price bleeds. Four confirmed wings + a dated catalyst
stack. Related: [[consumption-vs-investment-crux]], [[quiet-health-screen]], [[rotation-stickiness]].

> Firewall: DATA = verified/filed. THESIS = the capture read. Descriptive, not advisory.
