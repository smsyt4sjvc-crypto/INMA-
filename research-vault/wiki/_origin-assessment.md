  feedback loops. His whole mode.

  mechanical bid was a coin flip; prior-month momentum was noise), he modified the model, never abandoned it.
- **Edge Source = structural behaviors, not narratives (0.88)** ✓✓ — the session literally ARRIVED at "get

- **Self-Correction = fast (0.90)** ✓ — corrected Claude repeatedly ("capex needn't decelerate"; "I didn't

  200-day gate, margin off. Rule-bounded risk, exactly.
- **AI collab = directive & iterative; feedback = concrete/quantitative (0.90–0.92)** ✓ — drives, uses Claude
